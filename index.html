What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think, learn, and make decisions. AI spans various domains, including natural language processing, machine learning, robotics, and computer vision, transforming industries worldwide.

AI Integration in Healthcare

AI has revolutionized healthcare by enabling predictive analytics, personalized medicine, and enhanced diagnostics. For example, AI-powered tools can analyze medical imaging to identify potential health risks early, aiding doctors in making informed decisions and improving patient outcomes.

Applications of AI

AI finds applications across diverse fields, from automating mundane tasks in businesses to powering autonomous vehicles. In education, AI-driven tools create personalized learning experiences, while in entertainment, algorithms recommend content based on user preferences.
